Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from a course like Kaplan or Blueprint?

Those courses give everyone the same curriculum regardless of where they're actually struggling. Coaching starts with your specific diagnostic and builds the plan around your weak points, your schedule, and the resources you already own.

How much does coaching cost?

Pricing depends on your timeline and how much support you need — we'll go over exact numbers on your fit call after your application, once we understand your situation.

What if I don't hit my goal score?

If at any point coaching doesn't feel like it's working, you get a full refund — no questions asked. There's no risk in trying.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on your timeline and where you're starting from. We'll map out a realistic session cadence together after your diagnostic, based on your actual test date.

Do you work with students who are retaking the MCAT?

Yes — retakes are common, and often benefit the most from coaching, since we can dig into exactly what went wrong last time instead of repeating the same approach.

What if I haven't started studying yet, or I'm already deep into prep?

Either works. The plan is built from wherever you currently are — early in content review or already deep into practice — not from a fixed starting point.
